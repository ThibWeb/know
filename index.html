know.js — A simple tool to log stuff inside the console and beyond.

How to use it

▲ Bookmark ▶ This link to get the bookmarklet.

▼ Best viewed with your console open.

To open the console, try either ⌘ ⎇ I, ⌘ ⎇ K, Ctrl ⎇ K or F12 depending on your OS / browser.

You can then type and execute know.help() to get the API.

What's inside

  • IE8+, Webkit DevTools, Firefox DevTools, Firebug compatible.
  • Store logs in sessionStorage or localStorage, pick one.
  • Share logs with your coworkers via Gists (but beware of CORS).
  • Create a bookmarklet and display logs right into your browser window.
  • Logs are stored and shared in JSON and use an easy to parse easy to read syntax.
